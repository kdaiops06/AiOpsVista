Start your AI journey here — from machine learning basics to advanced topics like large language models and AI agents.

- **Natural Language Processing** — Text processing, embeddings, and language understanding

- **Prompt Engineering** — Getting the best results from AI models
- **Fine-Tuning** — Customizing models for your use case
- **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** — Grounding LLMs with your data
- **AI Agents** — Building autonomous agentic workflows
- **Responsible AI** — Ethics, bias, and fairness in AI systems
